About Me

Hi, I'm Maja! I'm 13 years old, currently in 8th grade, and attend McMillan Junior High School. I have three wonderful dogs that I love taking care of, cuddling with, and spending time with. I've always been passionate about dogs and enjoy helping them stay happy, healthy, and active.

Why Choose Me?

  • Responsibility: Pet owners can trust me to be on time and take good care of their dogs.
  • Experience: I currently have 3 dogs and have cared for others in the past, so I have great experience with older and younger dogs.
  • Fitness: I did gymnastics for 8 years, and now I do volleyball and dance, showing I have the energy and strength to care for your dog.
  • Genuine Love for Dogs: I treat every dog as if they were my own, ensuring they feel happy and cared for.

What Am I Doing This For?
